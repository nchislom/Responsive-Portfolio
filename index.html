About Me

I live and work in North Carolina with my wife and daughter. My hobbies include music, reading, D&D, gaming, calligraphy, and learning new technologies. I am also studying full-stack web development at the UNC Chapel Hill Coding Bootcamp. I am also a recording musician, live performer, and narrator. My music ranges from electro-acoustic to other cross-genre works using various contemporary, folk/old-world, and electronic music. I annually perform live as 'Keplar the Wanderer' at the Carolina Renaissance Festival, playing period as well as original works. I live in a house full of artists and I apparently do not sleep :-)
